 Canonical URL: https://schema.org/duration

The duration of the item (movie, audio recording, event, etc.) in ISO 8601 date format.
 

Values expected to be one of these types


Duration



Used on these types


Audiobook

Episode

Event

MediaObject

Movie

MusicRecording

MusicRelease

QuantitativeValueDistribution

Schedule
