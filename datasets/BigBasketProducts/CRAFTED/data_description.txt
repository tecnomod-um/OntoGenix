**Data Description:**
1. Absolute_Url: text - The absolute URL of the product - Any valid URL
2. Brand: text - The brand of the product - Any valid brand name
3. Category: text - The category of the product - Any valid category name
4. FAIR_URI: text - The FAIR URI of the product - Any valid URI
5. Image_Url: text - The image URL of the product - Any valid URL
6. ProductName: text - The name of the product - Any valid product name
7. Quantity: text - The quantity of the product in the invoice - Any valid quantity
8. SubCategory: text - The subcategory of the product - Any valid subcategory name
9. DiscountPrice: Numerical - The discounted price of the product in the invoice - Any valid price
10. Price: Numerical - The original price of the product - Any valid price
